Introduction

At MC Smart Home Connections, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including home security, utility, and home automation solutions.


Information We Collect

We gather various types of information to deliver, enhance, and personalize our smart home services.

  • Personal Information: Information such as your name, address, email, and phone number, collected when you register or subscribe to our services.
  • Service Usage Data: Data generated from your use of our security, utility, and automation services, including activity logs, device settings, and system notifications.
  • Device and Technical Data: Information about the devices you connect, including device types, IP addresses, and network details, to ensure proper function and troubleshooting.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to help us improve your experience and enable certain functionalities.


How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide, support, and enhance our services. We use your data to:

  • Operate and maintain our security, utility, and home automation systems.
  • Monitor and improve our service quality, functionality, and customer support.
  • Send you updates, security alerts, and important notifications related to your system.
  • Conduct troubleshooting, analysis, and optimization to improve our offerings.


Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is only shared with trusted partners for essential operational needs and with service providers who comply with our confidentiality and security standards. Legal obligations may also require us to disclose data under certain conditions.



Data Security

MC Smart Home Connections uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.



Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also opt-out of receiving promotional communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at myhometechadvisor@gmail.com.



Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
